Munich Database Meetup

We are a group of database enthusiasts, exploring a spectrum of databases, messaging systems, and technologies focused on storing and processing large amounts of data. This meetup is co-organized with TUMuchData Club.

What We’re About

Our community includes over 1,250 backend and distributed systems engineers and academics, and we host monthly meetups attracting 70-100 attendees. Together with TUMuchData, we aim to foster knowledge-sharing, networking, and hands-on experiences in the latest database and data-processing technologies.

Technologies We Cover

  • Cloud-Based: Cosmos DB, DynamoDB, Aurora, Snowflake, etc.
  • NewSQL: CockroachDB, VoltDB, NuoDB, etc.
  • NoSQL: Cassandra, Redis, MongoDB, Neo4j, etc.
  • OLAP: ClickHouse, Druid, Snowflake, etc.
  • OLTP: MySQL, Postgres, SQLite, etc.
